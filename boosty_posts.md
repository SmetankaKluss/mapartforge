🚀 **MapKluss — Minecraft Map Art Generator**

Hi! MapKluss is a free tool for creating map arts in Minecraft from any image.

The project was inspired by the idea of making life easier for builders and art creators — the process of crafting beautiful pixel paintings should be as convenient and accessible as possible.

🖼️ **What MapKluss can do:**
— Convert images into 2D (~61 colors) or 3D (~183 colors) art
— 7 dithering algorithms for best quality
— Export to LITEMATIC, PNG and MAP.DAT
— Works in browser, no registration needed

This channel will feature all new updates, improvements, and added features. Follow the project's development!
