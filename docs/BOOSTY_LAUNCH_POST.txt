ENG: 
MapKluss is launched! 🚀 mapkluss.art
Hi! I've launched MapKluss - a free map art generator for Minecraft that turns any image into a beautiful pixel painting for the game.
What MapKluss can do:
 • Converts images into 2D (flat ~61 colors) or 3D (stepped structure ~183 colors) arts
 • Supports 7 dithering algorithms for the best quality
 • Exports to LITEMATIC (for the Litematica mod), PNG, and MAP.DAT
 • Works entirely in the browser, no registration needed - just upload an image and you're done
The site is constantly evolving, and on this channel, I'll share all updates, new features, and improvements.
Your feedback is super important!
If you have ideas, suggestions for improvements, or spot any bugs - let me know:
 • Comments under posts
 • Direct messages in Telegram: @SmetankaKluss
Thanks for the support! Together, we'll make the best tool for creating map arts!
