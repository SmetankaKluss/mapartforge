# TikTok + YouTube Shorts Plan for MapKluss

This document is the practical launch plan for short-form video distribution for MapKluss.

## Goal

Use short-form video to:

- drive targeted Minecraft traffic,
- increase `image_uploaded`,
- increase `map_generated`,
- increase `litematic_exported` and `dat_exported`,
- validate which content angles bring builders instead of empty views.

## Platforms

Start with:

- YouTube Shorts
- TikTok

Optional later:

- VK Clips
- Instagram Reels

Do not split attention wider at the start.

## Posting cadence

For the first 30 days:

- 4 to 5 videos per week
- target: 20 videos in the first month

This is enough to get signal without spamming low-quality filler.

## Content formats

Keep everything inside four repeatable series.

### 1. Transformation

Format:

`source image -> MapKluss result -> export / in-game outcome`

Use for:

- anime
- logos
- memes
- photos
- pixel art

### 2. Comparison

Format:

- `2D vs 3D`
- `no dithering vs dithering`
- `legacy workflow vs MapKluss`

### 3. Micro-guide

Format:

- when to use 2D
- when 3D is worth it
- how to choose size
- when to avoid dithering

### 4. Build workflow

Format:

- preview -> materials -> litematic
- why pretty is not always buildable
- why 3D preview matters

## First 10 videos

1. Any image -> Minecraft map art in 30 seconds  
2. 2D Flat vs 3D Stair  
3. Clean logo conversion  
4. MapartCraft vs MapKluss  
5. Why materials matter  
6. Which dithering works for logos  
7. Anime art -> 3D Stair result  
8. Pixel art -> clean 2D result  
9. Litematic export workflow  
10. MAP.DAT export workflow

## Video structure

Ideal length:

- 20 to 35 seconds
- up to 45 when comparison actually needs it

Recommended structure:

1. first frame = strong visual result or strong comparison  
2. quick spoken or text hook in 1-2 seconds  
3. 2-4 steps on screen  
4. final result  
5. CTA

## CTA rules

Use:

- `mapkluss.art`
- `Try it on mapkluss.art`
- `Compare 2D and 3D yourself`

Avoid:

- begging for likes
- generic subscribe filler
- vague “link in bio guys” lines without a concrete reason

## What to measure

Views are secondary.

Primary:

- sessions by source / medium
- `image_uploaded`
- `map_generated`
- `litematic_exported`
- `dat_exported`

The question is:

Which videos bring actual builders, not just passive viewers?

## Production priorities

Do not start with facecam, big talking-head content, or lifestyle content.

Start with:

- screen capture of the product
- strong before/after
- captions
- focused hook
- one idea per video
